    Birth Certificate for Homelessness

    Based on the Section 103577 of the Health and Safety Code, individuals are provided with the opportunity for a fee-exempt copy of a birth record may be issued to an eligible homeless person, or homeless child or youth.

    Instructional Guide

    Below is a link to the form that needs to be filled out or provided:
