    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is this website and who is it for? +

    Many organizations provide services to aid the homeless community, but without proper information on how to receive help, it can be hard to find the right direction to take. Helping hands is a website that provides the necessary information for the homeless community to overcome challenges they are facing.

    What services are covered by this site? +

    The services covered on this website include a way to locate shelter/food banks/healthcare facilities, access to employement resources, and guides on how to fill out certification forms for organizations that can provide services like student homelessness, disabilitity, birth certificates, and a P.O. box.

    Are there helplines or hotlines I can call for support? +

    2-1-1 Sacramento is the primary provider of over-the-phone information for the Sacramento area. Dial 2-1-1 or 1-800-500-4931 for quick information on how to get help with emergency housing, food services, counseling, and/or employment. For those with hearing impairments, dial 7-1-1.

    Is my personal information safe? +

    Your information is safe with us, this is a non-profit website and we will NOT sell your data.
